Qi Li & Lychee's Final Team Project

For our final project, It's more about building website concerning the special camera "Fujifilm XT-30 II," which I am using for digital photography last semester.

Photography is similar to skiing, most people will always spend a lot on their pieces of equipment if they want to be more professional in this field. And also that is one of the reasons for me to build that website. It will help other people, who buy the camera from Fujifilm, to know which product will be a better fit for their situations; like different lenses, filters, tripods, and so on. And I will also present some of my recent photos to show the incredible landscape through my digital camera.

Besides, I will add some special skills for taking photos and appropriate Fujifilm XT-30 II Film Simulation Recipe. Hope people can get more professional advice on photography.
