Framing

A traditional way to direct attention to a subject is to frame it with something in the foreground. It enhances the subject as well as focusing our concentration on what the photographer deems important.

Symmetry

One of the easiest ways to create a perfect composition is to shoot a symmetrical subject. Tje photo is perfectly balanced, and the graphic design you end up with is usually very pleasing.

S-Curve

The S-curve can lead into or out of a scene,start at the bottom or side, but fundamentally it will roughly shape the letter S and either define the photograph or split the composition into two sections.

Still Life

Getting good at the various still life photography tips and techniques is the absolute fastest way to total mastery of photo techniques. We need to pay more attention on the lighting and the composition of placing.
